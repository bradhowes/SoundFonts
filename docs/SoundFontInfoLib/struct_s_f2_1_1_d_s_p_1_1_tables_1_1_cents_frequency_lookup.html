Detailed Description

Convert cent into frequency multiplier using a table lookup.

For instance, to reduce a frequency by -1200 cents means to drop 1 octave which is the same as multiplying the source frequency by 0.5. In the other direction an increase of 1200 cents should result in a multiplier of 2.0 to double the source frequency.
